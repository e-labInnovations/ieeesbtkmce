About IEEE

EEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.
Through its more than 427,000 members in more than 190 countries, widely referenced publications, conferences, technological standards, and professional and educational events, IEEE and its members encourage a worldwide community to create for a better tomorrow. IEEE is the world's most trusted "voice" for engineering, computer, and technology news.
